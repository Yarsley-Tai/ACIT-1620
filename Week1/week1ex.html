About Maimai

Maimai is an arcade rhythm game originating from Japan that has found success within the rhythm game market for its unique and innovative gameplay having garnered a very loyal playerbase not just from Japan but all around Asia.

Creators of Maimai

Maimai is a game created by SEGA a globally known video game company that is also known for other titles such as the Sonic and Yakuza game series. Maimai however was created by their Arcade game division.
