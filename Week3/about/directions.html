Directions to CIT Lounge Webville

From Joyce station take the Expo line skytrain heading to waterfront

Exit from the skytrain at Granville station or Waterfront station

Your destination is just a short walk away, less than 500 Metres in distance.

For Granville station walk towards the Dunsmair ST exit and you will be able to see the campus within your vicinity.

For Waterfront station walk upwards on the ST towards Granville st and you will find your destination on the right side of the street.
